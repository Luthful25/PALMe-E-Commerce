Frequently Asked Questions

Does PALMe have a store I can visit?
Yes, we have 4 outlets. Please order online or visit our shops directly.
Business Hours PST
Morning to midnight
Where do you ship?
We ship our Italian foods online all across the United States via UPS, including Hawaii and Alaska.
Does PALMe offer free shipping?
We are a small business and do not offer free shipping, but promo codes help reduce the cost.
What is your return policy?
For non-opened, non-perishable items, contact orders@palme.com within two business days to request a return label and refund.
Can I send gifts through PALMe?
Yes! We offer gourmet gift baskets and can send them to recipients. Only you will receive pricing and receipt info.
Does PALMe offer bulk quantities for catering?
Yes, order bulk essentials like pizza flour, olive oil, and Italian tomatoes online.
Is your website secure?
Your data is fully SSL-secured and never shared. Transactions are private.
